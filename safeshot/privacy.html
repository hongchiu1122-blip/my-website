SafeShot Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

SafeShot is a local-only Mac app for checking screenshots and images before sharing. Privacy is the core purpose of the product.

Short version: SafeShot processes images and OCR text locally on your Mac. It does not upload your images, OCR text, detection results, or redacted output. It does not require an account and does not use analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, or cloud OCR.
English 简体中文

English

1. Information SafeShot Processes

SafeShot may process the following information only on your Mac:

  • Images you provide from the clipboard, file picker, drag and drop, or the local macOS screenshot picker.
  • Text recognized from those images by Apple Vision OCR.
  • Local detection results, such as possible emails, phone numbers, URLs, passwords, API keys, tokens, verification codes, IP addresses, IDs, order numbers, QR codes, barcodes, faces, and manual redaction regions.
  • Redacted preview images and redacted output generated by the app.

2. Local Processing

All OCR, sensitive information detection, preview overlays, manual redaction, and image rendering happen locally on your Mac.

SafeShot uses Apple Vision for local OCR and deterministic local detection rules. SafeShot does not use remote OCR, cloud analysis, AI APIs, or any server-side image processing.

3. Information SafeShot Does Not Collect

SafeShot does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on a server. In particular, SafeShot does not collect:

  • Your original images.
  • OCR text recognized from your images.
  • Sensitive information detection results.
  • Manual redaction regions.
  • Redacted preview images or exported images.
  • Usage analytics, telemetry, advertising identifiers, or crash reports.
  • Account information, because SafeShot does not require an account or login.

4. What May Be Stored Locally

SafeShot may store simple app preferences locally on your Mac, such as:

  • App language.
  • OCR language preference.
  • Default redaction style.
  • Preview mode.
  • Enabled detection categories.
  • Whether to show the privacy footer.

These preferences do not include source images, OCR text, barcode payloads, manual redaction regions, or detection results.

5. Exported Files and Clipboard Output

SafeShot only writes a file when you explicitly choose to export a redacted PNG. SafeShot can also copy a redacted image to the macOS clipboard when you choose “Redact & Copy”. These actions are initiated by you and happen locally on your Mac.

6. Network, Analytics, and Tracking

SafeShot does not include network client functionality for uploading images or OCR text. SafeShot does not use analytics, telemetry, advertising, tracking, crash reporting SDKs, cloud sync, or remote OCR services.

7. App Sandbox and Permissions

SafeShot is designed for the macOS App Sandbox. It may request access to files you select through the system file picker or save panel. If you use local area capture, macOS may require Screen Recording permission for the local screenshot tool.

8. Detection Limitations

SafeShot uses OCR and local rule-based detection. It can help identify common sensitive information, but it cannot guarantee that every sensitive item will be found. You should still manually review names, faces, small text, and context before sharing an image.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as SafeShot evolves. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates the latest revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the developer through the support contact listed on the SafeShot App Store product page or the project page where this policy is hosted.

简体中文

1. SafeShot 处理的信息

SafeShot 只会在你的 Mac 本机处理以下信息：

  • 你通过剪贴板、文件选择器、拖拽或 macOS 本地截图工具提供的图片。
  • Apple Vision 在本机从图片中识别出的 OCR 文本。
  • 本地检测结果，例如可能的邮箱、手机号、链接、密码、API Key、Token、验证码、IP 地址、证件号、订单号、二维码、条形码、人脸区域以及手动打码区域。
  • 应用在本机生成的打码预览图和打码输出图。

2. 本地处理

SafeShot 的 OCR、敏感信息检测、预览标记、手动框选和打码渲染都在你的 Mac 本机完成。

SafeShot 使用 Apple Vision 进行本地 OCR，并使用确定性的本地规则进行检测。SafeShot 不使用远程 OCR、云端分析、AI API 或任何服务器端图片处理。

3. SafeShot 不收集的信息

SafeShot 不会在服务器上收集、传输或存储个人数据。具体来说，SafeShot 不会收集：

  • 你的原始图片。
  • 从图片中识别出的 OCR 文本。
  • 敏感信息检测结果。
  • 手动打码区域。
  • 打码预览图或导出的打码图片。
  • 使用分析、遥测、广告标识符或崩溃报告。
  • 账号信息，因为 SafeShot 不需要账号或登录。

4. 可能保存在本机的内容

SafeShot 可能会在你的 Mac 本机保存一些简单的应用偏好设置，例如：

  • 应用语言。
  • OCR 语言偏好。
  • 默认打码样式。
  • 预览模式。
  • 启用的检测类别。
  • 是否显示隐私提示。

这些偏好设置不包含原始图片、OCR 文本、二维码内容、手动打码区域或检测结果。

5. 导出文件和剪贴板输出

只有当你主动选择导出打码 PNG 时，SafeShot 才会写入文件。当你选择“打码并复制”时，SafeShot 也可以把打码后的图片复制到 macOS 剪贴板。这些操作都由你主动触发，并且只在本机完成。

6. 网络、分析统计和追踪

SafeShot 不包含用于上传图片或 OCR 文本的网络客户端功能。SafeShot 不使用分析统计、遥测、广告、追踪、崩溃报告 SDK、云同步或远程 OCR 服务。

7. App Sandbox 和权限

SafeShot 按 macOS App Sandbox 设计。它可能会访问你通过系统文件选择器或保存面板明确选择的文件。如果你使用本地区域截图功能，macOS 可能会要求为本地截图工具授予屏幕录制权限。

8. 检测限制

SafeShot 使用 OCR 和本地规则检测，可以帮助发现常见敏感信息，但无法保证识别所有敏感内容。分享图片前，你仍应手动检查姓名、头像、小字和上下文信息。

9. 隐私政策更新

随着 SafeShot 的更新，本隐私政策可能会调整。页面顶部的“Last updated”日期表示最近一次更新日期。

10. 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请通过 SafeShot App Store 产品页或托管本隐私政策的项目页面中列出的支持联系方式联系开发者。

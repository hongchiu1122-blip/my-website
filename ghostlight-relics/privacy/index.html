怪谈修复铺隐私政策

本隐私政策适用于 iOS 游戏《怪谈修复铺》（英文名：Ghostlight Relics，Bundle ID：com.heyhong.ghostlightrelics）。

1. 我们直接收集的信息

本游戏不要求注册账号，也不会主动要求你提供姓名、电子邮件、电话号码、通讯录、照片、麦克风、摄像头或精确位置。

游戏进度、资源数量、语言设置、触感反馈设置、广告奖励状态和免插屏购买状态会保存在你的设备本地，用于让游戏正常运行。除非你主动清除进度、卸载应用或系统清理数据，这些本地数据会保留在设备上。我们不会把这些本地存档上传到我们自己的服务器。

2. 内购和付款

本游戏通过 Apple App Store / StoreKit 处理应用内购买。付款信息由 Apple 处理，我们不会收到你的银行卡号、支付账号或完整付款信息。应用会接收购买结果、产品 ID 和交易状态，用于发放灵玉、补给包或免插屏权益。Apple 隐私政策：https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/。

3. 广告与第三方服务

本游戏接入 Google AdMob，用于展示激励广告和插屏广告。Google 及其合作方可能会处理设备标识符（例如广告标识符，如你允许）、IP 地址、广告展示和互动记录、应用互动数据、崩溃或性能诊断数据等信息，用于广告投放、广告衡量、防欺诈和服务维护。

在 iOS 上，游戏会请求 App Tracking Transparency（ATT）授权，用于展示更相关的广告。拒绝授权不会影响正常游玩，也不会影响你自愿观看激励广告获取奖励；广告可能变为非个性化、上下文广告或受限广告。Google 隐私政策：https://policies.google.com/privacy。

4. 数据共享

我们不会出售你的个人信息。为实现游戏功能、广告变现、内购结算、故障排查或遵守法律要求，相关数据可能由 Apple、Google AdMob 及其服务提供商处理。

5. 你的选择和权利

你可以在 iOS 设置中更改“允许 App 请求跟踪”或本游戏的跟踪授权状态。你也可以在游戏设置中切换语言、触感反馈或重置进度。内购恢复通过游戏内“恢复购买”入口完成。根据你所在地区的法律，你也可以联系我们请求访问、更正或删除与你相关的数据。

6. 儿童隐私

本游戏并非面向 13 岁以下儿童或当地法律规定的未成年人同意年龄以下用户。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。如果你认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请通过本文末尾的联系方式联系我们。

7. 跨境处理

Apple、Google 或其服务提供商可能会在你所在国家或地区以外处理数据。我们会依据适用法律和平台政策处理相关事项。

8. 政策更新

如果游戏功能、广告、内购或数据处理方式发生变化，我们可能更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本会发布在同一页面，并标明新的生效日期。

9. 联系我们

如你对本隐私政策或数据处理有疑问，请联系：hong@heyhong.net

Ghostlight Relics Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS game Ghostlight Relics, also localized as 怪谈修复铺 / 怪談修復鋪, Bundle ID com.heyhong.ghostlightrelics.

1. Information We Collect Directly

The game does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone access, camera access, or precise location.

Game progress, resource balances, language settings, haptic settings, ad reward state, and no-interstitial purchase state are stored locally on your device so the game can function. These local records remain on your device until you reset progress, uninstall the app, or the system clears the data. We do not upload these local save files to our own server.

2. In-App Purchases and Payments

In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store / StoreKit. Apple processes payment information. We do not receive your payment card number, payment account, or full payment details. The app receives purchase results, product IDs, and transaction status so it can grant Jade, supply packs, or the no-interstitial entitlement. Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The game uses Google AdMob to show rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Google and its partners may process device identifiers, such as the advertising identifier if you allow it, IP address, ad impressions and interactions, app interaction data, crash data, and performance diagnostics. These data may be used for ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and service maintenance.

On iOS, the game requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission to show more relevant ads. If you decline, you can still play normally and voluntarily watch rewarded ads for rewards. Ads may become non-personalized, contextual, or limited ads. Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. To provide game functionality, advertising, in-app purchase settlement, troubleshooting, or legal compliance, relevant data may be processed by Apple, Google AdMob, and their service providers.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can change "Allow Apps to Request to Track" or this game's tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also switch language, haptics, or reset progress inside the game settings. Purchase restoration is available through the in-game "Restore Purchases" entry. Depending on your region, you may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of data related to you.

6. Children's Privacy

The game is not directed to children under 13 or under the age of consent required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the contact method below.

7. International Processing

Apple, Google, or their service providers may process data outside your country or region. We handle such matters according to applicable laws and platform policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the game's features, ads, in-app purchases, or data practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact: hong@heyhong.net

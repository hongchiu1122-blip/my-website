Cat Stars 隐私政策

最近更新：2026-06-02

本隐私政策适用于 iOS 游戏 Cat Stars（中文名：猫咪方格）。我们重视用户隐私，并尽量减少对个人信息的处理。

1. 我们收集的信息

Cat Stars 是一款单机逻辑解谜游戏。游戏不要求注册账号，不收集姓名、邮箱、电话、通讯录、照片、精确定位等个人身份信息。

游戏进度、金币、关卡记录、设置项、皮肤和主题等数据主要保存在你的设备本地，用于恢复游戏状态和提供基础游戏功能。

2. 广告与第三方服务

本应用接入 Google AdMob，用于展示可选的激励广告。用户可以选择观看广告来获得复活、提示、抽奖或奖励加成；不观看广告也可以继续体验核心游戏内容。

Google AdMob 可能会根据其政策处理设备信息、广告标识符、广告互动信息、粗略位置或诊断数据，用于广告投放、频次控制、反作弊、统计和广告归因等目的。具体请参阅 Google 的隐私政策：

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. 追踪与广告标识符

如果系统要求授权 App Tracking Transparency（ATT），我们会按照 iOS 系统提示征得你的许可。你可以在 iOS“设置”中随时管理是否允许应用请求追踪。

4. 权限使用

本应用不申请相机、麦克风、相册、通讯录、精确定位等敏感权限。应用可能使用系统触感反馈能力，用于操作反馈和游戏体验。

5. 数据存储与删除

保存在本地的游戏数据会随应用一起存储在设备上。你可以通过删除应用来移除本地游戏数据。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用面向普通休闲游戏用户，不主动收集儿童的个人信息。如果你认为我们意外处理了儿童个人信息，请通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

7. 政策更新

如本政策有更新，我们会在此页面发布新版本，并更新“最近更新”日期。

8. 联系我们

Cat Stars Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS game Cat Stars. We respect user privacy and aim to minimize the processing of personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Cat Stars is a single-player offline logic puzzle game. The app does not require account registration and does not collect personal identity information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

Game progress, coins, level records, settings, skins, and themes are mainly stored locally on your device to restore game state and provide core gameplay features.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. Users may choose to watch ads to receive revives, hints, spins, or reward bonuses. Core gameplay remains available without watching ads.

Google AdMob may process device information, advertising identifiers, ad interaction information, approximate location, or diagnostic data according to its own policies for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, analytics, and attribution. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Tracking and Advertising Identifier

If App Tracking Transparency (ATT) authorization is required by the system, the app will request your permission through the iOS system prompt. You can manage tracking permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

4. Permissions

The app does not request access to camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or precise location. The app may use system haptic feedback for game interactions.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Local game data is stored on your device. You can remove local game data by deleting the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general casual game users and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have unintentionally processed children’s personal information, please contact us using the email below.
